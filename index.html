The routine

Four steps, sixty seconds

The full guide ships on the back of every tray — here's the short version.

01

Pluck a cluster

Lift one fanned cluster straight up from the tray using tweezers.

02

Dip the base

Touch the root into a small dot of lash glue — a little goes a long way.

03

Place it

Set the cluster just above your natural lash line, angled outward.

04

Hold and go

Press for five seconds while the glue sets. Repeat until you're happy.
